About Us

How we Make Change?

At Silaawat, we believe fashion should be more than just style—it should tell a story, empower communities, and create a lasting impact. Founded in 2025, Silaawat is a brand by women, for women, designed to celebrate creativity while providing livelihood opportunities by skilling the women artisans.

Our journey began with the incredible young girls we met in aftercare institutes—girls with immense talent and a passion for creating beautiful handcrafted products. Inspired by their resilience, we built Silaawat as a platform for empowerment, helping them gain financial independence and recognition for their artistry.

More than just a fashion label, Silaawat is a movement—a blend of heritage hand embroidery and contemporary fashion, making everyday wear unique, colorful, and full of cultural significance.

One Family, One Purpose

At Silaawat, we are more than a brand — we are a sisterhood. Our unique model brings together two powerful roles in our creative journey:

Nayikas – the leaders who build and guide local embroidery circles

Sahelis – the artisans who create beauty with every stitch

Nayikas (नायिका) – The Embroidery Heroines

Nayikas are leaders, organizers, and changemakers. They bring Silaawat to life in new places by:

Starting their own Silaawat embroidery circle

Receiving designs, materials, and training from us

Forming and guiding a team of Sahelis

Managing quality, timelines, and production

Earning as they grow their unit and impact

🌿 A Nayika is the heart of her community — graceful, strong, and driven.

Example: Meera from Bhopal becomes a Nayika. She forms a team of 5 women in her locality, mentors them, and oversees the production of Silaawat designs with her unique touch.

Sahelis (सहेली) – The Artisan Sisters

Sahelis are skilled women artisans who join the Silaawat family to do what they love — embroidery. They:

Receive assigned patterns and guidance

Work from home or in a small group

Get fairly paid for their work

Grow their skills and confidence

Add personal love to every stitch

🌿 A Saheli is a friend, an artist, a dream-weaver — the soul of our designs.

Example: A group of 4 women in Delhi joins as Sahelis. They focus on embroidery, working at their own pace and contributing to high-quality handcrafted products.
